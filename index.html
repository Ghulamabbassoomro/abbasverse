Who is Ghulam Abbas Khan Soomro?

He is a multidisciplinary creator operating fluidly across industrial cinema environments and contemporary visual spaces. Now rooted in Germany pursuing advanced specializations in International Media Cultural Work at Hochschule Darmstadt, his creative footprint expands into premium fashion editorials, text composition, acting performance, and cultural media preservation.
